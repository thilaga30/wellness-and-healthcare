💊 Women's Health Issues

ℹ️ Disclaimer: This information is for educational purposes only. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for diagnosis and treatment.
🔴

PCOS — Polycystic Ovary Syndrome

Symptoms
  • Irregular or missed periods
  • Excess hair growth (face, body)
  • Acne and oily skin
  • Weight gain, especially around abdomen
  • Thinning hair or hair loss
  • Difficulty getting pregnant
Management Tips
  • Maintain a healthy weight through diet and exercise
  • Eat low-glycemic index foods
  • Exercise regularly (30 min/day)
  • Manage stress with yoga and meditation
  • Consult a gynecologist for hormonal treatment
Prevention
  • Avoid processed foods and sugar
  • Maintain healthy BMI
  • Regular health check-ups
🩸

Anemia — Iron Deficiency

Symptoms
  • Constant fatigue and weakness
  • Pale skin and nails
  • Shortness of breath
  • Dizziness and headaches
  • Cold hands and feet
  • Brittle nails and hair loss
Management Tips
  • Eat iron-rich foods (spinach, lentils, red meat)
  • Take iron supplements as prescribed
  • Pair iron with Vitamin C for better absorption
  • Avoid tea/coffee with meals (blocks iron)
  • Get regular blood tests (CBC)
Prevention
  • Include iron-rich foods in every meal
  • Regular hemoglobin checks
  • Treat heavy periods promptly
🦋

Thyroid Disorders

Symptoms (Hypothyroid)
  • Unexplained weight gain
  • Fatigue and sluggishness
  • Feeling cold all the time
  • Dry skin and hair loss
  • Depression and brain fog
  • Irregular periods
Management Tips
  • Take prescribed thyroid medication consistently
  • Eat iodine-rich foods (seafood, iodized salt)
  • Avoid goitrogens in excess (raw cabbage, soy)
  • Exercise regularly to boost metabolism
  • Get TSH levels checked every 6 months
Prevention
  • Adequate iodine intake
  • Avoid environmental toxins
  • Annual thyroid screening after 35
🧠

Stress & Mental Wellness

Symptoms
  • Persistent anxiety and worry
  • Sleep disturbances (insomnia)
  • Headaches and muscle tension
  • Irritability and mood swings
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Digestive issues (IBS)
Management Tips
  • Practice daily meditation (10 min)
  • Exercise regularly — releases endorphins
  • Maintain a consistent sleep schedule
  • Talk to a therapist or counselor
  • Limit social media and screen time
Prevention
  • Build strong social support network
  • Set healthy work-life boundaries
  • Practice gratitude journaling

🏥 When to See a Doctor
🚨 Seek Immediate Help
  • Severe chest pain or difficulty breathing
  • Heavy vaginal bleeding (soaking pad hourly)
  • Sudden severe abdominal pain
  • High fever with pelvic pain
  • Signs of miscarriage during pregnancy
  • Severe dizziness or fainting
📅 Schedule an Appointment
  • Periods absent for 3+ months
  • Unusual discharge or odor
  • Persistent fatigue despite rest
  • Unexplained weight changes
  • Mood changes lasting weeks
  • Annual gynecological check-up
⚠️ Remember: Early detection saves lives. Don't ignore persistent symptoms. Regular check-ups are essential for every woman.
