🧘 Yoga & Wellness

🌿 Yoga Tip: Practice on an empty stomach, wear comfortable clothing, and breathe deeply throughout each pose. Listen to your body and never force a stretch.

🩸 Period Yoga

These gentle poses help relieve cramps, reduce bloating, and ease period discomfort naturally.

🐍

Cobra Pose (Bhujangasana)

A gentle backbend that stretches the abdomen, relieves uterine cramps, and improves blood flow to the pelvic region.

  • Lie face down, palms under shoulders
  • Inhale and slowly lift your chest off the floor
  • Keep elbows slightly bent, shoulders relaxed
  • Hold for 15–30 seconds, then release
Relieves cramps Improves posture Reduces fatigue
🧸

Child's Pose (Balasana)

A deeply restorative pose that gently stretches the lower back and hips, providing instant relief from period cramps and back pain.

  • Kneel on the floor, big toes touching
  • Sit back on your heels and fold forward
  • Extend arms forward or rest alongside body
  • Breathe deeply and hold for 1–3 minutes
Back pain relief Calms mind Reduces bloating
🦋

Butterfly Pose (Baddha Konasana)

Opens the hips and groin, stimulates the ovaries and uterus, and helps relieve menstrual discomfort and irregular cycles.

  • Sit with spine straight, soles of feet together
  • Hold feet with both hands, knees out to sides
  • Gently flap knees up and down like butterfly wings
  • Hold still for 1–2 minutes, breathing deeply
Hip opener Hormonal balance Pelvic relief

🤰 Pregnancy Yoga

Safe, gentle poses for each trimester. Always consult your doctor before starting prenatal yoga.

🐄

Cat-Cow Stretch (Marjaryasana)

A flowing movement between two poses that relieves back pain, improves spinal flexibility, and is safe throughout all trimesters.

  • Start on hands and knees (tabletop position)
  • Inhale: drop belly, lift head and tailbone (Cow)
  • Exhale: round spine, tuck chin and tailbone (Cat)
  • Flow between poses for 1–2 minutes
Back pain relief Spinal flexibility Safe all trimesters
🧘

Seated Forward Bend (Paschimottanasana)

Gently stretches the hamstrings and lower back. Calms the nervous system and reduces anxiety during pregnancy.

  • Sit with legs extended straight in front
  • Inhale and lengthen your spine upward
  • Exhale and gently fold forward from hips
  • Hold for 30–60 seconds, breathing deeply
Reduces anxiety Hamstring stretch Calms mind
🌬️

Pranayama Breathing (Anulom Vilom)

Alternate nostril breathing that reduces stress, improves oxygen supply to baby, and prepares the body and mind for labor.

  • Sit comfortably with spine straight
  • Close right nostril, inhale through left for 4 counts
  • Close left nostril, exhale through right for 4 counts
  • Repeat 5–10 cycles, focusing on breath
Stress reduction Better oxygen flow Labor preparation

☀️ General Wellness Yoga

Daily yoga practices for overall health, energy, and mental clarity. Suitable for all women.

☀️

Surya Namaskar (Sun Salutation)

A complete 12-step sequence that works every muscle group, boosts metabolism, improves circulation, and energizes the entire body.

  • Start in prayer pose (Pranamasana)
  • Flow through 12 poses in sequence
  • Coordinate each movement with breath
  • Complete 5–10 rounds for full benefit
Full body workout Boosts metabolism Improves energy
🌳

Tree Pose (Vrikshasana)

A balancing pose that strengthens the legs and core, improves concentration and focus, and builds mental stability and confidence.

  • Stand tall, shift weight to left foot
  • Place right foot on inner left thigh or calf
  • Bring palms together at chest or raise overhead
  • Hold for 30–60 seconds, then switch sides
Balance & focus Leg strength Mental clarity
🧘

Mindfulness Meditation

A simple seated meditation practice that reduces cortisol levels, improves emotional regulation, and promotes deep inner peace and clarity.

  • Sit comfortably with eyes closed
  • Focus attention on your natural breath
  • When mind wanders, gently return to breath
  • Practice for 5–20 minutes daily
Reduces stress Better sleep Emotional balance
