    🥗 Pregnancy Diet Guide

    EAT THESE

    • Folic acid rich foods (spinach, lentils)
    • Iron-rich foods (red meat, beans)
    • Calcium sources (milk, yogurt, cheese)
    • Omega-3 (salmon, walnuts, flaxseed)
    • Whole grains (oats, brown rice)
    • Fresh fruits and vegetables
    • Lean protein (chicken, eggs, tofu)
    • Plenty of water (10+ glasses)

    AVOID THESE

    • Raw or undercooked meat/fish
    • Unpasteurized dairy products
    • High-mercury fish (shark, swordfish)
    • Alcohol and smoking
    • Excess caffeine (limit to 200mg/day)
    • Raw sprouts and unwashed produce
    • Processed and junk food
    • Artificial sweeteners in excess

    📖 Trimester Guide

    🌱 First Trimester (Weeks 1–12)

    Baby's organs begin forming. You may experience morning sickness, fatigue, and breast tenderness. Take folic acid daily. Avoid alcohol and smoking. Schedule your first prenatal visit.

    🌸 Second Trimester (Weeks 13–26)

    Most women feel better now. Baby starts moving! You'll have more energy. Focus on iron and calcium intake. Start prenatal yoga. Baby's gender can be determined around week 20.

    🌺 Third Trimester (Weeks 27–40)

    Baby grows rapidly. You may feel back pain and shortness of breath. Prepare your hospital bag. Practice breathing exercises. Watch for signs of labor. Rest as much as possible.
