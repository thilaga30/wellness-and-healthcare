🥗 Diet & Nutrition

🥦 Nutrition Reminder: A balanced diet rich in iron, calcium, and folate is essential for women's health at every stage.
🤰 Pregnancy Diet

EAT THESE

  • Folic acid (spinach, lentils, fortified cereals)
  • Iron (red meat, beans, tofu, pumpkin seeds)
  • Calcium (milk, yogurt, cheese, broccoli)
  • Omega-3 (salmon, sardines, walnuts)
  • Vitamin D (eggs, fortified milk, sunlight)
  • Whole grains (oats, quinoa, brown rice)
  • Fresh fruits (berries, oranges, mangoes)
  • Lean protein (chicken, eggs, legumes)

AVOID THESE

  • Raw/undercooked meat and eggs
  • High-mercury fish (shark, king mackerel)
  • Unpasteurized cheeses and juices
  • Alcohol (no safe amount in pregnancy)
  • Excess caffeine (max 200mg/day)
  • Deli meats and hot dogs (listeria risk)
  • Raw sprouts (bacterial contamination)
  • Artificial sweeteners in excess
🩸 Period Diet

EAT THESE

  • Iron-rich foods (spinach, lentils, red meat)
  • Magnesium (dark chocolate, almonds, avocado)
  • Omega-3 (flaxseed, chia seeds, salmon)
  • Ginger and turmeric (anti-inflammatory)
  • Bananas (potassium, reduces bloating)
  • Chamomile and ginger tea
  • Complex carbs (sweet potato, oats)
  • Vitamin C (citrus, bell peppers)

AVOID THESE

  • Salty snacks (increase water retention)
  • Caffeine (worsens cramps and anxiety)
  • Alcohol (disrupts hormones)
  • Sugary foods (blood sugar spikes)
  • Fried and processed foods
  • Carbonated drinks (worsen bloating)
  • Excess dairy (can increase prostaglandins)
  • Spicy foods if sensitive
🌿 General Women's Diet
✅ Daily Essentials
  • 🥬 Leafy greens (spinach, kale)
  • 🫐 Berries (antioxidants)
  • 🥚 Eggs (protein + vitamin D)
  • 🥛 Dairy or fortified alternatives
  • 🌾 Whole grains (fiber)
  • 🥜 Nuts and seeds (healthy fats)
  • 🐟 Fatty fish (omega-3)
  • 💧 8+ glasses of water
❌ Always Avoid
  • 🍟 Deep fried foods
  • 🍭 Excess sugar and sweets
  • 🥤 Sugary sodas and juices
  • 🍕 Ultra-processed foods
  • 🧂 Excess sodium (salt)
  • 🍺 Alcohol
  • ☕ Excess caffeine (3+ cups)
  • 🍬 Artificial sweeteners daily
💊 Key Nutrients
  • 🩸 Iron — prevents anemia
  • 🦴 Calcium — bone health
  • 🌿 Folate — cell growth
  • ☀️ Vitamin D — immunity
  • 🐟 Omega-3 — heart health
  • 🥦 Magnesium — muscle function
  • 🍊 Vitamin C — iron absorption
  • 🫀 B12 — nerve health

🏃‍♀️ Exercise & Wellness
🚶‍♀️
Daily Walking
30 minutes of brisk walking improves circulation, mood, and metabolism. Best done in the morning.
🧘‍♀️
Yoga & Stretching
15–20 minutes of yoga reduces stress, improves flexibility, and balances hormones naturally.
💪
Strength Training
Light resistance training 2–3x/week builds bone density and muscle strength. Essential for women over 30.
🍽️ Sample Daily Meal Plan
🌅 Morning Routine
  • 🌊 Wake up: 1 glass warm water with lemon
  • 🧘 7:00 AM: 15 min yoga or stretching
  • 🥣 8:00 AM: Oats with fruits and nuts
  • ☕ 9:00 AM: Green tea or herbal tea
  • 🍎 11:00 AM: Mid-morning snack (fruit)
🌙 Evening Routine
  • 🥗 1:00 PM: Balanced lunch with protein + veggies
  • 🥜 4:00 PM: Healthy snack (nuts, yogurt)
  • 🚶 5:00 PM: 30-min walk or exercise
  • 🍲 7:30 PM: Light dinner (soup, salad, protein)
  • 🌙 9:00 PM: Warm milk with turmeric
💧 Water Reminder
💧 Daily Goal: Drink at least 8–10 glasses (2–2.5 liters) of water daily. During pregnancy, increase to 10–12 glasses. During periods, warm water helps reduce cramps.
